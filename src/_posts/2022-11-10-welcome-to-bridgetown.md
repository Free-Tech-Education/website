<div class="my-4">Below is a Ruby snippet which asks your name and prints it:-</div>

```ruby
def print_hi(name)
  puts "Hi, #{name}"
end
print_hi('Chetan Mittal')
#=> prints 'Hi, Tom' to STDOUT.
```
